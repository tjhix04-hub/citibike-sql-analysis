# CitiBike Ridership Analysis with SQL

# Project overview 
This project analyzes CitiBike trip data using SQL to explore rider usage, and operational trends

## Tools 
- SQL 
- GitHub

#  Questions Explored 
### How many total ride are in this dataset?
The dataset contains about 1,000,000 total rides in 2022. 

### How many rides were taken by casual riders vs. members?
Member riders account for the majority of trips. this indicates that the subscirption based users are riding more often than casuals. 

### What are the top 10 busiest start stations 
The busiest rides are concentrated in Manhattan, with the top station, 'W 21 St & 6 Ave', having 5787 rides. 
This suggests that the ridership demand is highly centralized around key commercial and commuting areas. 

### What is the average trip duration for casual vs. member riders?
Casual riders have more than double average trip duration. 
This suggests that member riders are more likely using CitiBike for commuting or routine travel, while casual riders may be using the service for more leisure-oriented trips.

#Business Questions

### Does CitiBike need more electric bikes or standard bikes? 
Classic bikes are more prominent in the share of CitiBike rides, indcating a higher demand for the classic bikes. 
CitiBike should consider putting money towards investing in more standard bikes to improve rider satisfaction

### How can CitiBike convert casual riders to members?
CitiBike already does an amazing job with this, with almost 9x more more member rides than casual, but there is stil room to grow. 
Member riders make up the majoriy of customers, but on average, casual riders take twice as much time as members. 
This suggests casual riders are using CitiBike for extended leisure trips and may represent a strong opportunity for membership conversion. 
CitiBike could offer targeted promotions, such as discounted trial memberships or subscription incentives, to encourage casual riders to become recurring members and increase long-term revenue.

### What times of the day should CitiBike have more bikes available? 
Rides peak during the 4 PM and 5 PM hour. CitiBike should ensure the ample number of bikes to ensure rider satisfaction and experience. 

## Key Takeaways

- CitiBike demand is highly concentrated in specific stations and peak commute hours
- Member riders generate the majority of trips, but casual riders represent strong conversion potential
- Casual riders take longer trips, suggesting leisure usage and opportunity for targeted membership promotions
- Operational efficiency can be improved by reallocating bikes to high-demand stations and peak hours
